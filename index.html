ABOUT US

We are a specialist electrical installation contractor delivering the full range of electrical and ICT services throughout the country. Our focus is on becoming your preferred electrical contractor and equally on the retention and enhancement of our traditional reputation for delivering good value, total trustworthiness and excellent installation quality time after time.

AMS is a privately owned Sheffield based Electrical Installation Contractor originally founded by Managing Director, Shaun Finney. Shaun, whose previous background spanned Electrical Apprentice right through to Senior Contracts Manager, was later joined by Director Christopher Hanson to further enhance the existing management team. Chris, who now heads up the Design and Estimating Departments of the business, has worked alongside Shaun for the past 18 years and has also enjoyed considerable hands-on experience within the electrical contracting industry.

Our Associations

All of our site staff hold CSCS (Construction Skills Certification Scheme) accreditation, UKATA asbestos awareness training certification and a number of electricians are fully trained to work in 'live' asbestos removal enclosures, an area crucial to many asbestos abatement schemes having worked alongside national specialists.

To reflect this commitment to health and safety at work the company holds accreditation from the NICEIC, (the National Inspection Council for Electrical Installation Contracting), CHAS (The Contractors Health and Safety Assessment Scheme) and Constructionline.

OUR SERVICES

CAD & Design Services

Our in-house computer aided design department provides a full professional design service including lighting calculations, design schedules, co-ordination drawings, schematic diagrams, fully detailed layout drawings, cable calculations, specifications and performance briefs.

Electrical Installations

Industrial and commercial installation service, throughout the UK, is the Company's core business. As a NICEIC approved Contractor we can provide a wealth of experience of professional and cost effective engineering solutions to meet the most exacting requirements.

Testing & Inspection

With health and safety regulations becoming ever more stringent, and insurance companies demanding more rigorous conditions before they offer cover, it's vital that electrical installations are properly maintained. AMS is an NICEIC-approved contractor, meaning we have the skilled resources that modern businesses require. Our experienced team understand electrical testing regulations and are fully qualified to carry out comprehensive periodic inspections to ensure your premises and appliances are fully compliant at all times.

Data Installations

Whether you want to upgrade your existing data cabling structure, or require a complete new data cabling installation need, we have a trained specialist team at your disposal. Combined electrical services / data design and installation turnkey packages are available.

Fire & Security Systems

We specialize in the design, installation and maintenance of high-quality, electronic security and fire systems for sites and establishments where the highest levels of protection are required.

Small Works & Maintenance

Our fully equipped maintenance and small works division offers electrical maintenance, repair and small work services for every type of business; be it retail, commercial, education, leisure, healthcare and/or industrial, planned or reactive. With highly trained and experienced engineers and a dedicated office support team, AMS guarantees a prompt, professional service at competitive prices.

Project Management

We now offer standalone Project Management and Consultancy services nationwide. From advising architects to comprehensive design and cost analysis, we provide tailored electrical solutions. Our expertise is adaptable to fit your needs, ensuring quality and cost-effectiveness every step of the way.
